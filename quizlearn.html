Quiz: What type of learner are you?


Home Learning Style Quiz Study Routine Quiz Paper or Electrical?

1. When I relax, I tend to...

Watch tv, movie, or go to a museum
Play music , listen to the radio, or talk with a friend
Read or write a story or a poem
Work out, play a sport, make crafts, or build something

2. I best understand when a teacher or presenter...

Uses diagrams, charts, and graphs
Does Q&A and holds a group discussion
Gives out worksheets and readings
Does demonstrations or model examples

3. If you did poorly on a test, you would like feedback in form the of ...

A graph or chart showing the topics you did well on and did not do well on
Having a teacher talk it through with you
A detailed written explanation of what you need to improve on
Usage of examples from what you have done (the correct way/answer from the questions you got wrong)

4. If you were lost, how would you want someone to give you directions?

Have them describe the landmarks and buildings they'll see
Have them tell you the street names or the names of the landmarks
Have them write down the street names
Have them take you to the location

5. If you have to create a project of any format, which format would you choose?

Make a poster
Write a speech
Write a persuasive essay or research paper
Make a model

6. When I am determining someone's mood, I mostly

Use their facial expressions to determine their mood
Listen to their tone of voice to determine their mood
Analyze what they are saying to determine their mood
Look at their body language to determine their mood

7. When I don't know how to spell a word, I'm most likely going to ...

Visualize the letters in my mind
Sound it out loud
Write it down and determine if it looks right
Draw the letters out in the air

8. You have been diagnosed with a complex disorder. Your prefer for your doctor to

Show you a diagram of what's wrong
Have him/her talk it through with you
Give you some info packet to read about your disorder
Use a model to show you what is wrong

Reading resources are good for visual and reading/writing learners.
Video sources are good for auditory learners as well as visual learners.

Results 0-5 You're a Visual Learner
Results 6-10 You're an Auditory Learner
Results 11-15 You're a Reading/Writing Learner
Results 16-20 You're a Kinesthetic Learner
