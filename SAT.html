Resources for SAT prep:
  • Prep Scholar:
    Get to know about the SAT before you take the test; this site comes with scheduling help and tips on how to do well and improve your score.
  • College Board:
    Tells the user everything they need to know about the SAT. Lists SAT testing dates, allows students to register, gives tips to taking the SAT and guides to interpret the score. Contains practice tests.
  • The Best Schools:
    This website contains FAQ about the SAT as well as gives you ten websites that will help you get prepared.
  • Kaplan:
    This site gives free SAT practice, as well as 20-minute workouts and quizzes.
  • BWS Education:
    This site contains SAT vocabulary guide, practice reading, writng, and math tests.
  • Princeton Review:
    This site tells you all you need about the SAT.
  • Khan Academy:
    This site gives you personalized SAT practice based on your previous SAT score or your PSAT score and gives you up to 8 practice tests.
  • College Raptor:
    This site gives you tips for dealing with the anxiety that comes with preparing for test day.
